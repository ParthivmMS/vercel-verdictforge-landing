VerdictForge

Simplify. Summarize. Succeed.

VerdictForge is an AI-powered legal judgment summarization tool built by law students in India. It converts complex Indian court judgments into easy-to-read summaries in both legal and plain English formats. Law students and lawyers can save time and understand case law faster using this tool. The backend is powered by OpenRouter + Mistral models, hosted on Render. This frontend is SEO-optimized and built on Vercel. Visit our AI summarizer to try it for free.
